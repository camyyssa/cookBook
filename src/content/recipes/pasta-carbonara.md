---
title: Pasta Carbonara
description: Creamy Roman classic with guanciale, egg, and pecorino.
servings: 4
prepTime: 10 min
cookTime: 20 min
ingredients:
  - 400g spaghetti
  - 200g guanciale (or pancetta)
  - 4 egg yolks
  - 1 whole egg
  - 100g pecorino romano, finely grated
  - Black pepper
tags:
  - pasta
  - italian
  - quick
---

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
2. Cut the guanciale into small strips. Cook in a cold pan over medium heat until crispy and the fat has rendered. Remove from heat.
3. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, whole egg, and most of the pecorino. Add plenty of black pepper.
4. When the pasta is ready, reserve a cup of pasta water. Drain and add the pasta to the guanciale pan (off the heat).
5. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta and toss vigorously, adding splashes of pasta water until you get a creamy sauce.
6. Serve immediately with the remaining pecorino and more black pepper.
