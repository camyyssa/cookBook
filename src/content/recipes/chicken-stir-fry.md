---
title: Chicken Stir-Fry
description: Fast weeknight dinner with whatever vegetables you have.
servings: 2
prepTime: 15 min
cookTime: 10 min
ingredients:
  - 2 chicken breasts, sliced thin
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 1 tbsp cornstarch
  - 1 bell pepper, sliced
  - 1 cup broccoli florets
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  - Vegetable oil for cooking
  - Rice to serve
tags:
  - asian
  - quick
  - weeknight
---

1. Toss the sliced chicken with soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Let it sit while you prep the vegetables.
2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
3. Cook the chicken in a single layer, without stirring, for 2 minutes. Flip and cook another 2 minutes. Remove from the pan.
4. Add the vegetables, garlic, and ginger. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until crisp-tender.
5. Return the chicken to the pan, toss everything together, and serve over rice.
