---
title: Easy Baked Cabbage
description: Thick cabbage wedges roasted at high heat until caramelised.
prepTime: 5 min
cookTime: 20 min
ingredients:
  - 1 head of cabbage
  - Olive oil
  - Salt and pepper
tags:
  - vegetables
  - simple
  - vegan
---

1. Preheat oven to 275°C.
2. Cut the cabbage into thick wedges, keeping the core intact so they hold together.
3. Drizzle olive oil on both sides and season generously with salt and pepper.
4. Place on a baking tray and bake for 10 minutes per side (20 minutes total).
