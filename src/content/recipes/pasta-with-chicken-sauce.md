---
title: Pasta with Chicken Sauce
description: Creamy lemon chicken pasta with garlic. No onions.
prepTime: 10 min
cookTime: 20 min
ingredients:
  - Pasta
  - Chicken breast, cut into pieces
  - 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  - Cream for cooking
  - 1 lemon (juice and zest)
  - Salt and pepper
  - Olive oil
tags:
  - pasta
  - chicken
  - quick
---

1. Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper and cook until golden, about 5-6 minutes. Set aside.
3. In the same pan, sauté the garlic for 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Pour in the cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Add lemon juice and zest.
5. Return the chicken to the pan and stir to coat. Simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
6. Add the drained pasta and toss, adding a splash of pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
7. Taste, adjust seasoning, and serve immediately.
