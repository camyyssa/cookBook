---
title: Pasta with Lemon Chicken Sauce
description: Creamy lemon chicken pasta with garlic. No onions.
image: ../../assets/images/pasta-with-chicken-sauce.png
prepTime: 10 min
cookTime: 20 min
ingredients:
  - Pasta
  - Chicken breast, cut into pieces
  - 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  - Cream for cooking
  - 1 lemon
  - Salt and pepper
  - Olive oil
tags:
  - pasta
  - chicken
  - quick
  - main
rating: 5
---

1. Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper and cook until golden, about 5-6 minutes. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Pour in the cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Add lemon juice. Simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
4. Add the drained pasta and toss, adding a splash of pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
