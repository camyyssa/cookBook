---
title: Lentil Soup
description: Hearty and warming, ready in under an hour.
servings: 6
prepTime: 10 min
cookTime: 40 min
ingredients:
  - 300g red lentils
  - 1 onion, diced
  - 2 carrots, diced
  - 2 celery stalks, diced
  - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  - 1.5L vegetable broth
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - Olive oil
  - Salt and pepper
  - Lemon juice to serve
tags:
  - soup
  - vegan
  - healthy
---

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Sauté onion, carrots, and celery until soft, about 5 minutes.
2. Add garlic, cumin, and paprika. Stir for one minute until fragrant.
3. Add the lentils, crushed tomatoes, and broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
4. Cook for 30-35 minutes until the lentils are completely soft.
5. Blend partially with an immersion blender for a thick but chunky texture (or leave as-is).
6. Season with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice before serving.
